v2 Corrections

What was wrong in v1 and what was fixed:

Fixed in v2

Playfair Display serif headings removed — was completely wrong. All headings now Nunito 800/900.
Stat numbers font changed from Playfair Display to Caveat (handwritten/sketch) — matches reference exactly.
Teal color corrected from dark #3B7A8C to pastel #6AAAB8.
Orange corrected from #E8845A to warmer #F07838.
Topbar corrected from dark navy to powder blue #D8E8EF.
Photo blob frame now uses light steel-blue (#BECFDA) not cream.
About section now shows orange blob behind photo (key BubaKids pattern).
Card icons changed from filled squares to teal circle outlines.

Unchanged

Orange CTA buttons — pill shape, bold, same concept
Inter for body copy and UI elements
Dashed decorative rings and SVG curve lines
Cream/beige stats bar background
Organic blob shapes for photo frames
Two-column hero split layout
Overall section structure and spacing rhythm
